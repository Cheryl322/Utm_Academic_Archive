## 🧠 Formative Reflection: Design Thinking Framework

### 1. Key Insights Gained
The **Empathize phase** within the Design Thinking framework has radically shifted my system-design mindset. Prioritizing user empathy allowed our team to clearly define root user problems before rushing into development, resulting in more purposeful low-fidelity prototypes and efficient testing phases. Additionally, the fast-paced, collaborative nature of the framework proved that structured teamwork drastically shortens troubleshooting cycles and refines overall solution quality.

### 2. Industry Alignment & Future Improvements
To solidify my potential in a highly competitive tech landscape, I need to balance these structural design methodologies with advanced technical execution:
*   **Hard Skills Acceleration:** I intend to pursue targeted short-term certifications via Coursera and LinkedIn to deepen my core programming languages, specifically in **SQL, C++, and Java**. These hard skills are crucial for translating abstract design prototypes into robust production-ready code.
*   **Soft Skills & Synergy:** Because modern enterprise software engineering relies on cross-functional team collaboration, smooth interpersonal communication is vital. I am actively seeking out multi-team group initiatives to foster positive team synergy, minimize delivery friction, and consistently output superior technical products.
