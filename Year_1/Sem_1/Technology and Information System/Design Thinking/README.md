## Reflection: Design Thinking Framework

### 1. Key Insights Gained
The Design Thinking framework has deeply enhanced my **problem-solving skills** as a software engineering student. Specifically, the **Empathize phase** taught me that true problem-solving begins with user empathy; by clearly defining user friction points first, we can build purposeful low-fidelity prototypes and systematically test our solutions. Additionally, applying this framework within a team setup proved that collaborative problem-solving significantly accelerates troubleshooting cycles and elevates final solution quality.

### 2. Industry Alignment & Future Improvements
To maximize my potential in the technology industry, I need to balance these problem-solving frameworks with strong technical execution:
*   **Hard Skills Optimization:** I plan to pursue short-term specialized certifications via Coursera and LinkedIn to advance my hard skills in **SQL, C++, and Java**. These technical capabilities are essential for turning abstract prototypes into high-performance, production-ready code.
*   **Soft Skills & Synergy:** Since modern technology projects rely heavily on cross-functional team collaboration, smooth interpersonal communication is critical. I will actively seek out opportunities to participate in group projects to refine my soft skills, foster great team synergy, and ultimately deliver higher-quality software products.
