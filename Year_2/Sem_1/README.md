## 🧠 Semester Reflection & Gained Knowledge

### 1. Key Insights Gained
This semester represented a critical scaling phase in my academic journey, transitioning from foundational programming into complex software engineering and data management. Mastering *Data Structures and Algorithms* fundamentally upgraded my core code optimization skills, changing how I evaluate script runtime efficiency. At the same time, *Database* and *System Development Technology* allowed me to bridge backend storage optimization directly with cross-platform application execution. Finally, *Software Engineering* gave me a deep appreciation for collaborative development lifecycles, proving that engineering high-quality technology requires both flawless structural design maps and structured team communication.

### 2. Actionable Suggestions for Improvement
Reflecting on the rigorous workloads this term, managing multiple cross-functional group projects simultaneously could be streamlined further by adopting formalized project tracking ecosystems. Incorporating collaborative industry toolsets (such as tracking feature backlogs and sprint boards via Jira or Trello) into our early team development stages would heavily reduce project friction and better prepare us for enterprise engineering environments.
